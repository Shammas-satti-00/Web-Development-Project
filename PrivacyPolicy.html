Access to Account and Profiles

For ease of access to your account, you open a session when you log in to the website. This uses technology that enables us to provide direct account access and to help administer service without requiring any password or other user identification to be re-entered when your browser revisits the service within a given time frame. To remove access to your account, actively log out from on each device. Where possible, users of public or shared devices should log out at the completion of each visit. If you sell or return a computer, you should log out and deactivate the device before doing so. If you do not maintain the security of your password or device, or fail to log out or deactivate your device, subsequent users may be able to access your account, including your personal information. If you share or otherwise allow others to have access to your account, they will be able to see your information (including in some cases personal information) such as your search history, reviews and account information (including your email address).
